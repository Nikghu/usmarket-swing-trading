| SRD-EXE-007.005 | FO-EXE-007 | Must | **`_on_realtime_bar(symbol, bar)` — tick processing:** Called by the IBKR callback on every 5-second bar. Processing steps in order: (1) Acquire `_lock`. (4) Compute `window_start = _floor_3m(bar.datetime)` — floor `bar.datetime` (ET) to the nearest 3-minute boundary, then convert to UTC. (9) Emit `candle_updated(symbol, self._partials[symbol])`. | `symbol: str`, `bar: RealtimeBar` (from `IBKRClient`) | updated `PartialBar`; `candle_updated` signal; `candle_closed` signal on window boundary | Lock must be held only for steps 1–8; signal emission (step 9) happens outside the lock to avoid GUI deadlock | Draft |

| SRD-EXE-007.007 | FO-EXE-007 | Must | **RTH guard — `_is_rth(dt: datetime) -> bool`:** Returns `True` only if `dt`, converted to US/Eastern time, is on a weekday and falls within `09:30:00 ≤ dt_et < 16:00:00`. Use `zoneinfo.ZoneInfo("America/New_York")` for conversion. At or after 16:00:00 ET: `_on_realtime_bar()` discards the incoming tick (returns early); additionally a `QTimer` running every 60 seconds calls `_check_session_end()` which, once `_et_now() >= 16:00:00`, iterates `_partials`, calls `_close_bar` for any partial with `tick_count > 0` to avoid losing the last candle partially formed before close.
